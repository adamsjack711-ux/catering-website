Peg's Story

"How Hard
Can It Be?"

Peg started cooking at age 8 with an apple pie, and never really stopped. While her mother worked full-time, Peg became the family's head chef — a role she took on with the same fearless curiosity that's defined her ever since.

After years in retail and restaurant management, she opened Juniper Cafe & Bistro in Edmonton — the perfect training ground for large-scale cooking and baking. In 2019, she relocated to Kamloops and shifted her focus to what she loves most: cooking for people who appreciate real food, made with care.

"Just eat something!!"
